💧 What do I do if my iPhone gets wet?

1. CALL The iPhone Guy IMMEDIATELY – 559-471-5837 🆘

2. DO NOT PLUG IT IN. Turn it off. Power kills wet circuits.

3. Dry it, then drop it in a bag of rice or uncooked Top Ramen (yes, seriously).

4. Let it chill 24–48 hrs, then call us for the real fix.

📴 iPhone blacked out but still rings or glitching?

It's not dead — it's just stuck. Try this HARD RESET:

  1. Tap Volume Up (quickly)
  2. Tap Volume Down (quickly)
  3. Hold down the Power button and KEEP HOLDING
  4. Wait for that Apple logo like your life depends on it

If it keeps doing this, hit up The iPhone Guy. We’ll fix the root issue. 🔧
